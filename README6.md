# 6. Strange Ways (Creative Writing)

In these strange ways, sometimes people are careless, sometimes oblivious. Sometimes, someone loves boundlessly, while others are immersed in intense disdain. Sometimes, someone surrenders at the gates of hardship. Sometimes, a trusting hand is embraced tightly. Sometimes, someone enters another’s emptiness and leaves it emptier. Yet, these strange ways are calculated; one day, everything aligns, completing the pattern of life.
